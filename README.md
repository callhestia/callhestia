## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tarikul-Islam-Anik/Animated-Fluent-Emojis/master/Emojis/Objects/Laptop.png" width="25"> About me

I am a student of **Digital Information Processing** at the **Jagiellonian University in Krakow**, believing in learning by doing. 

I am the founder of **[Gonsior](https://github.com/GonsiorHack)** — an ambitious group of students joining forces to build real-world projects. Recently, we participated in **HackYeah 2025**, where we built **[Reign of Abaddon](https://github.com/callhestia/Reign-of-Abaddon.git)**.

**My focus:** Expanding the Gonsior organization, bringing together young minds, competing in hackathons, and gaining hands-on industry experience.

**Open Source:** I actively contribute to documentation for open-source projects like **[Ladybird Browser](https://github.com/LadybirdBrowser)** and **[Tauri](https://github.com/tauri-apps)**.

## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tarikul-Islam-Anik/Animated-Fluent-Emojis/master/Emojis/Travel%20and%20places/Star.png" width="25"> Featured Projects

**[🚗 ZaWahę – Intelligent Fuel Ecosystem](https://github.com/callhestia/zawahe-mobile-app-design.git)**
A comprehensive UI/UX concept for a mobile platform solving information asymmetry in the fuel market. Designed in Figma using Atomic Design methodology. It features a "One-Tap Update" for crowdsourced live prices, Route Efficiency mapping, and EV readiness. 

**[🏥 Honorata Dawid – Nursing Services](https://github.com/callhestia/strona-dzialalnosci-pielegniarskiej.git)**
A professional, responsive medical SPA built with **SvelteKit (Svelte 5)**, **TypeScript**, and **Tailwind CSS**. Optimized for elderly users with High-Contrast UI, featuring a Formspree-integrated reservation system, smooth Intersection Observer animations, and Vercel deployment.
